1. Road Construction

Title: “Built-to-Last Roads, End-to-End”

Road milling and asphalt laying from TRAI give clients a complete solution for renewing, resurfacing and upgrading roads with minimal disruption and long-term performance in mind. This applies whether the project involves profiling the existing surface and overlaying, or fully removing old damaged asphalt and installing a brand-new pavement structure.

Integrated milling and resurfacing benefits

  • Precise correction of levels and drainage
  • Controlled milling removes only the required depth, allowing TRAI to correct crossfalls, eliminate ruts and restore proper water run-off so roads drain efficiently and last longer.
  • Smoother, safer ride quality
  • Careful profiling followed by high-standard asphalt laying delivers a uniform, even surface that improves vehicle comfort, reduces splash and spray, and enhances skid resistance for all road users.
  • Life extension of existing pavements
  • By keeping the sound base layers and renewing only the worn surface, TRAI can significantly extend pavement life without the cost and time of full reconstruction.

Full removal and new asphalt construction benefits

  • Complete renewal of failed surfaces
  • Where asphalt is badly cracked, deformed or patched, removing the old layer and replacing it with new mix eliminates underlying defects instead of simply hiding them.
  • Opportunity to upgrade design and materials
  • Reconstruction allows TRAI to adjust layer thicknesses, choose more durable mixes and incorporate additives or recycled content that improve long-term performance and sustainability.
  • Cleaner appearance and better asset value
  • Fresh, uniform asphalt with crisp edges and markings immediately lifts the visual quality of roads, car parks and access routes, improving the perceived value of the whole development.

Shared benefits when choosing TRAI

  • Reduced disruption and faster programs
  • Combining milling and paving in a coordinated operation shortens closure times and phasing, helping clients keep traffic flowing and businesses accessible during works.
  • Cost-effective, sustainable solution
  • Milled material can be processed for reuse in new asphalt, reducing waste and material costs while supporting environmental targets.
  • Single specialist responsible for quality
  • Using TRAI for both removal and new laying gives clients one accountable partner for design levels, mix selection, compaction and finish, simplifying communication and aftercare.

Kerbstone and interlock installation - Key Benefits

Kerbstone and interlock installation from TRAI completes the road construction story by combining structural control with a high-quality architectural finish around the carriageway. Clients get edges, pavements and paved areas that are aligned to design levels, built for heavy use and visually consistent with the rest of the project.

  • Engineered edges for safer roads
  • Kerbstones are set to precise lines and levels, helping to contain the pavement, protect verges and manage surface water so the asphalt performs as designed. Strong, correctly founded kerbs also guide drivers and define safe pedestrian areas, improving overall road safety and legibility.
  • Durable, flexible interlock pavements
  • Interlocking blocks distribute loads across many units, creating a robust surface that resists cracking and rutting under traffic and temperature changes. Individual units can be lifted and replaced if services need access or local damage occurs, making maintenance faster and more economical than full-depth repairs.
  • Premium visual finish and place-making
  • A wide choice of block colours, textures and patterns allows TRAI to align pavements, medians, plazas and parking bays with the client’s landscape and branding vision. Clean kerb lines and well-cut interlock patterns give projects a polished, “finished” look that enhances curb appeal and the perceived value of the entire development.
  • Integrated with the full road build
  • Because TRAI manages milling, asphalt laying, kerbs and interlock together, levels, joints and transitions are coordinated from the outset, avoiding ponding, trip lips and awkward steps. One experienced road contractor taking responsibility for the whole cross-section means smoother delivery, fewer interface issues and long-term performance that matches the design intent.

2. Road Marking Application

Title: “Lines That Lead the Way”

Clear, long-lasting markings are critical for safe, intuitive navigation on any network. TRAI Contracting specifies and applies the right marking system for each site, balancing durability, visibility, cost and environmental performance.

  • Thermoplastic road markings: Thermoplastic markings offer exceptional life and reflectivity on heavily trafficked roads and highways. Glass beads and anti-skid aggregates can be incorporated to maximize night-time visibility and braking performance in wet conditions.
  • 2K MMA (cold plastic) systems: Cold plastic MMA markings are used in high-stress areas such as intersections, roundabouts, bus lanes and cycle routes where superior strength is needed. Their high film thickness, colour stability and skid resistance provide reliable performance even under aggressive braking and turning.
  • Cold paint and specialist line marking: Cold-applied paints are ideal for car parks, airports, logistics yards and sports areas that require fast application and minimal downtime. Using advanced spray equipment, layouts are set out and applied with precision to optimize space and traffic flow.

Thermoplastic Marking – Key Benefits

Hot Applied Road Marking (Thermoplastic Road Marking or Pavement Marking) from TRAI completes the road construction story by pairing a high-quality surface with long-life, high-visibility guidance for every road user. It is ideal for highways, city streets, car parks and special zones where safety, durability and professional presentation matter most.

  • Maximum safety and visibility
  • Thick, reflective thermoplastic lines and symbols stand out in day, night and wet conditions, giving drivers more time to see lanes, crossings and warnings and react safely. Retroreflective glass beads and optional profiled markings enhance night-time guidance and can create rumble effects that encourage drivers to slow down in critical areas.
  • Long-lasting, low-maintenance markings
  • Hot-applied thermoplastic bonds strongly to asphalt and primed concrete, forming a robust layer that resists wear from heavy traffic, braking and extreme weather far better than conventional paint. This extended service life reduces the frequency of remarking, cutting future maintenance costs and limiting the need for repeated traffic closures.
  • Fast application, less disruption
  • Once applied at the correct temperature, thermoplastic cools and hardens within minutes, allowing roads to reopen quickly and keeping disruption to residents, businesses and through-traffic to a minimum.
  • Versatile layouts for any project
  • The material can be used for center and edge lines, arrows, symbols, speed roundels, crossings, cycle lanes, bus lanes, parking bays and traffic-calming features, giving TRAI the flexibility to deliver complete pavement marking packages on complex schemes.
  • End-to-end professionalism from a road specialist
  • Because TRAI also handles milling, asphalt laying, kerbs and interlock, levels and layouts are coordinated so markings sit exactly where designers intended—clear of joints, ponding and defects. This integrated road construction expertise means clients get crisp, accurate, regulation-compliant markings that look professional from day one and continue to perform throughout the life of the pavement.

2K MMA (cold plastic) markings from TRAI provide ultra-durable, high-visibility guidance in the most demanding locations, backed by full road-construction expertise and specialist product training. This service is ideal for junctions, roundabouts, crossings, bus and cycle lanes, high-traffic approaches and any area where conventional paint wears out too quickly.

  • Strong colour stability and retro-reflectivity keep markings bright and legible for longer, helping drivers read lanes, arrows and symbols clearly at night and in poor weather.
  • Cold-cure chemistry allows application in a wider range of temperatures than hot-applied systems, supporting reliable programs and shorter closures throughout the year.
  • Exceptional mechanical strength and abrasion resistance, ideal for stress areas such as roundabouts, intersections, bus lanes and braking zones.
  • Very high durability in harsh climates, maintaining colour, thickness and clarity under heavy tires, heat and UV exposure.
  • Fast chemical curing at a wide range of temperatures, enabling application in cooler conditions and reducing downtime.
  • Outstanding colour stability and retro-reflectivity, keeping markings bright and readable for longer periods.
  • Superior skid resistance due to high-build thickness and embedded aggregates, improving safety for vehicles, cyclists and pedestrians.
  • Flexible design options: can be textured, profiled, multi-coloured or inlayed to create rumble effects, cycle lanes, logos and safety zones.
  • Lower maintenance frequency compared with standard paints, making it attractive for asset owners focused on whole-life performance.

Integrated with TRAI’s road construction expertise

Specialist manufacturer support and market edge

4. RO & TDP Design, Traffic Management & TMA

Title: “Safe Works, Flowing Traffic”

Well-planned traffic management protects people, assets and programs whenever works take place on live networks. TRAI Contracting offers a complete service from early design and authority approvals to fully managed solutions on site.

  • RO & TDP design and approval: Experienced planners prepare Road Opening (RO) and Traffic Diversion Plan (TDP) submissions that meet authority standards and local regulations. Layouts are engineered to maintain capacity, safeguard vulnerable users and keep emergency access clear while works progress.
  • End-to-end traffic management services: Temporary signs, signals, barriers and lane closures are designed, installed and monitored by trained operatives working to recognized safety schemes. Continuous supervision and inspection ensure that arrangements remain effective as traffic patterns and site conditions change.
  • TMA (Truck-Mounted Attenuator) rental: For high-risk locations, TRAI Contracting provides impact protection vehicles fitted with certified attenuator systems. These mobile crash cushions reduce the forces in the event of a collision, shielding both work crews and road users.
  • Events, utilities and emergency works: Rapid-response teams support short-notice interventions for utilities, incident response and special events, minimizing disruption to the public. Integrated communication with clients, authorities and stakeholders helps deliver safe, predictable outcomes on every operation.

Road Opening (RO) - Key Benefits

  • Ensures legal compliance by formally authorizing who can open the road, where, when and for how long, reducing regulatory and liability risk.
  • Improves safety for workers, drivers and pedestrians by requiring approved traffic management layouts, signage and protection before works begin.
  • Protects the road asset by specifying reinstatement standards, so trenches and patches are restored to a quality that preserves pavement life.
  • Minimizes disruption through clear timing, phasing and coordination with other utilities or projects, helping to avoid repeated works in the same location.
  • Provides traceability and accountability, with records of the party responsible for the opening and the condition in which the carriageway was handed back.

TDP Design & Approval - Key Benefits

  • Enhances safety by carefully planning lane closures, diversions, crossings and signage to reduce conflict points and crash risk.
  • Reduces congestion and delay through optimized routes, phasing and signal timings, keeping journey times as short and reliable as possible.
  • Protects vulnerable users by incorporating safe walking and cycling routes, clear crossings and speed management in affected areas.
  • Supports regulatory compliance and faster approvals by documenting that the design follows recognized road-safety and traffic-engineering standards.
  • Improves communication and coordination between contractors, utilities and authorities, helping to avoid clashes between projects and repeated disruptions.
  • Delivers better community outcomes by minimizing noise, pollution and access issues while maintaining reliable access to homes, businesses and emergency services.

Renting Truck-Mounted Attenuators (TMAs) - Key Benefits

  • Enhanced worker and driver safety by placing an energy-absorbing crash cushion between live traffic and the work crew, significantly reducing the severity of rear-end impacts.
  • Compliance with modern work-zone safety standards by using properly specified, tested and professionally set-up TMA units on high-speed or high-risk roads.
  • Lower upfront costs compared with purchasing dedicated TMA trucks, with flexible rental terms that match project duration and budget.
  • Reduced downtime and maintenance risk, as TRAI manages inspections, servicing and post-impact checks so clients always receive road-ready equipment.
  • Operational flexibility to move protection between jobs and locations, ideal for mobile lane closures, striping, sweeping and other moving operations.
  • Protection of valuable equipment and work vehicles ahead of the TMA, helping to limit property damage and associated repair or delay costs in the event of a collision.

6. Epoxy Flooring & Coatings

Title: “Seamless Floors, Heavy-Duty Performance”

Epoxy systems from TRAI Contracting transform concrete surfaces into seamless, high-performance floors that stand up to demanding use. These solutions combine durability, chemical resistance and design flexibility for industrial, commercial and public environments.

  • Industrial epoxy coatings: High-build epoxies protect floors in warehouses, plants, workshops and logistics centers from abrasion, impact and chemical attack. Smooth, dust-free surfaces improve housekeeping, reflect light and support safe, efficient operations.
  • Decorative and commercial epoxy: Coloured, flake and self-levelling systems create a refined, contemporary look for showrooms, retail spaces, offices and public buildings. Bespoke colours, zoning and graphics can be incorporated to reinforce branding and guide foot traffic.
  • Anti-slip and safety systems: Textured finishes and aggregate-loaded topcoats are applied in wet, oily or sloped areas to reduce slip risk. Marked walkways, hazard zones and parking bays help organizations comply with safety standards and improve site legibility.
  • Maintenance, repair and upgrades: Existing epoxy and concrete floors are surveyed, prepared and refurbished to extend life and minimize downtime. TRAI Contracting works with clients to schedule phased upgrades, keeping facilities operational while quality improvements are delivered.

Epoxy Flooring – Key Benefits

  • Epoxy flooring delivers a strong, seamless and low-maintenance surface that performs exceptionally well in demanding commercial and industrial environments. It combines durability, chemical resistance and design flexibility, making it ideal for everything from warehouses and plant rooms to car parks and public facilities.
  • High durability and impact resistance to withstand heavy traffic, machinery and frequent vehicle loading without rapid wear.
  • Excellent chemical and stain resistance against oils, fuels, solvents and cleaning agents, protecting the underlying concrete slab.
  • Seamless, non-porous finish that is easy to clean, improves hygiene and reduces dust build-up compared with bare concrete or tile joints.
  • Enhanced safety through slip-resistant aggregates and the option to mark walkways, safety zones and parking bays directly into the floor.
  • Highly customizable appearance, available in a wide range of colours, gloss levels and decorative effects to match branding or zoning needs.
  • Long service life and reduced maintenance, lowering lifecycle costs and downtime for busy facilities.
